1. What is Phishing?

Phishing is a social engineering attack where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal credentials, OTPs, or financial information.

2. How to Recognize Phishing Emails

  • Unknown or spoofed sender
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Spelling and grammar errors
  • Suspicious links or attachments

3. How to Identify Fake Websites

  • Look-alike domain names
  • Missing HTTPS
  • Unnecessary login requests

4. Social Engineering Techniques

  • Fear and urgency
  • Authority impersonation
  • Fake rewards

5. Best Practices

  • Never share passwords or OTPs
  • Verify sender identity
  • Enable multi-factor authentication
  • Report suspicious emails
